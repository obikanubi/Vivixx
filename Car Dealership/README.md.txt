Use this to purchase a car from Dan's Toyota Car shop.
As of July 7, 2017 the "Customize Car" and "Car Tracker" menu options are still unavailable.

To run program:
1. Open up your terminal and make sure you are in the directory where this program is stored.
2. Type "Ruby menuex.rb" to open the program.

To use: 
1. Simply use the number pad and press enter to navigate through the menu.
2. Remember to proceed to the checkout menu to finalize your purchase before exiting the program.
